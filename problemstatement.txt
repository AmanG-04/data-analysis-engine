DATA ANALYTICS & INSIGHTS

Build a data engine that turns raw information into business decisions.

Focus Areas
1. Decision Models
2. Market Research
3. Churn Prediction

Suggested Problem Directions
1. Customer segmentation using behavioral and transaction data.
2. Funnel analytics to identify drop-off points and conversion levers.
3. Churn risk prediction with actionable retention triggers.
4. Market intelligence from external and internal datasets.

What Good Solutions Should Show
1. Clear problem framing and measurable outcomes.
2. Reliable data processing pipeline and reproducible results.
3. Insight delivery that supports real business decisions.
4. A practical path to deployment in fintech or financial services contexts.

EVALUATION CRITERIA

Projects are evaluated by the Blostem core team across five dimensions. The weights guide scoring, but standout strength in one dimension can improve overall assessment.

1. Relevance to Brief (25%)
Does the solution directly solve the stated challenge? Is it specific to fintech, financial services, or your assigned track?

2. Technical Execution (25%)
Is the system real, functional, and deployable? Are code quality, architecture, and implementation discipline strong?

3. Innovation and Thinking (20%)
Does the project introduce a non-obvious approach or original perspective rather than minor iteration?

4. Demo and Narrative (20%)
Can the team clearly communicate what was built, why it matters, and how it works through a concise, polished demo?

5. Potential and Scale (10%)
If funded, could this solution scale in India and address a meaningful market problem?

Submission Tip
A working, deployable product with a focused story usually outperforms an ambitious but incomplete codebase. Ship first, then polish.
