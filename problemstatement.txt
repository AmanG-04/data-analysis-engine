Evaluation:
Projects are evaluated by the Blostem core team on five dimensions. These are weighted but not mechanical — a standout performance in one area can lift the overall score.

Relevance to Brief
25%
Does your solution directly address the challenge? Is it specific to fintech, financial services, or your assigned track?

Technical Execution
25%
Is the code real and working? Is the architecture sound? Did you deploy it? Code quality, system design, and commit history matter.

Innovation & Thinking
20%
Is this a genuinely new angle? Non-obvious approaches and systems thinking are rewarded over incremental tweaks.

Demo & Narrative
20%
Can you explain what you built, why it matters, and how it works? A polished, clear demo is half the battle.

Potential & Scale
10%
If funded, could it scale? Does it address a real problem with genuine market size in India?

Tip: A working deployed app with a tight narrative beats an impressive codebase that nobody can run. Ship first, polish second.
